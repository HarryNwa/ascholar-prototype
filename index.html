The Assessment

Four rounds. One trusted result.

Each cohort runs through up to four progressive rounds. Students are ranked on a Progression Table that universities and sponsors can access directly — no cold applications, no guesswork.

Who It's For

Built for every side of the equation.

AScholar serves four groups — each with a distinct role in turning academic potential into real opportunity.

🎓
Students

High-potential students seeking international scholarships and university places — regardless of access to expensive preparation or guidance.

  • Sit free, standardised assessments
  • Build a verified academic profile
  • Receive direct offers and invitations
  • Access learning content and exam prep
🏛️
Universities & Admission Officers

Institutions looking to discover academically ready international candidates — without the noise of unverified cold applications.

  • Access the ranked Progression Table
  • Filter by country, subject, score range
  • Send invitations or admission offers
  • Zero cost for founding partners
💰
Scholarship Sponsors

Organisations and philanthropists who want to fund the right students — with verified merit data, not just compelling essays.

  • Discover candidates by track and score
  • Issue scholarship offers directly
  • Invite applications with confidence
  • Full audit trail on all offers
📚
Teachers & Educators

Subject experts who create the content that prepares students for the assessment — and earn revenue as their teaching reaches a global audience.

  • Publish courses, notes, and videos
  • Run subject-specific class threads
  • Earn from student engagement
  • Shape how students prepare
